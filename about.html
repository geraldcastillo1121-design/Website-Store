ABOUT US

DC Monorack is a motorcycle bracket brand that aims to provide high-quality motorcycle brackets at an affordable price. We believe that every motorcycle rider deserves a reliable and durable bracket to enhance their riding experience. Our products are designed and manufactured with precision and care, using only the best materials and techniques. We are committed to delivering excellent customer service and support, ensuring that our customers are satisfied with their purchase. At DC Monorack, we are passionate about motorcycles and strive to be the go-to brand for all motorcycle bracket needs.

HISTORY OF THE BRAND

DC Monorack is established in 2017, DC Monorack began with a single man, Jomar Dela Cruz. From a one-man team he began to recruit friends and relatives, even offering training for those with little to no experience in making brackets. Believing in hard work and paying it forward, has led DC Monorack to have a family-like bond. Currently, the brand continues to grow, both locally and soon internationally.
